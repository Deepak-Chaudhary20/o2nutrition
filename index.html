About O 2 Founder

Sumit Pal Singh is a male nutrition expert based in India who has gained a reputation for his expertise in the field of nutrition and dietetics. With a focus on evidence-based nutrition and personalized counseling, Sumit has helped countless individuals achieve their health and fitness goals through targeted nutrition interventions and education.

  • Sumit has a Bachelor's degree in Nutrition and Dietetics from Punjab University, and a Master's degree in Sports Nutrition from Lovely Professional University. He is also a Certified Sports Nutritionist from the International Society of Sports Nutrition (ISSN), and a Certified Diabetes Educator from the Indian Association of Diabetes Educators (IADE).
  • In addition to sports nutrition, Sumit also provides counseling and education on a range of other nutrition-related issues, including weight management, diabetes, heart health, and digestive health. He uses a personalized approach, taking into account the individual's health history, lifestyle, and dietary habits, to develop a nutrition plan that is tailored to their specific needs and goals.
  • Sumit is also a prolific writer and speaker, having written articles on nutrition and health for various publications and websites, and presented at conferences and seminars on topics such as sports nutrition, weight management, and disease prevention. He is passionate about educating and empowering individuals to take control of their health through proper nutrition and lifestyle choices.
